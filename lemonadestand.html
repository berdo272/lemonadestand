The game will start by having you purchase your first inventory of supplies.
Use the table above to plan your purchases to ensure you do not run out of supplies during the day!
You will then be shown the forcasted weather for the current day. People are more willing to buy lemonade on hotter days!
Next you will be prompted to choose which type of lemonade you would like to make, and set your price.
Keep in mind that your ice will melt at the end of each day, and any unsold cups from the day will be tossed aswell.
Try to make as much money as you can! Remember to keep prices resonable and pay attention to your weather!
